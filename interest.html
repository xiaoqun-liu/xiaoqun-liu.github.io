Research Interests

Natural Language Processing

Natural Language Processing (NLP) is the study of how to enable computers to understand and process natural human language. I am interested in the theory and application of NLP in various fields, such as large language model(LLM) safety, their inner construction and LLM compression.

Linguistic

Linguistics is the scientific study of human language, involving carefully observing, collecting, and analyzing language data to uncover patterns and principles. I'm specifically interested in the intersection of philosophy and computer science, namely Linguistic.

Evolutionary Computation

Evolutionary Computation is a computational method based on the principles of biological evolution for solving complex problems and optimization tasks. I mainly interests in the application of evolutionary algorithms(EA) in optimization, machine learning, and data mining.

Online Learning

Consequences that data overview is hidden and arrives in a stream, the model must adapt to the data distribution change. Online learning allows it to learn and update incrementally as it receives new data. I'm interested in the nexus of online learning and EAs.

Memrister Computing

A computational model and calculation method based on the Memristor, an electronic component with nonlinear and dynamic resistance characteristics. I mainly focus on simulates the memristor crossbar in the computer and the application of memristor computing in machine learning and optimization.
